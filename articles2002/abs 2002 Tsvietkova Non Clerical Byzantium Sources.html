Citation author: Y.Tsvietkova.

Bibliography: Kyiv National University n.a. T.Shevchenko Bulletin Legal Sciences, issue 47, 2002 pp.208-212

Abstract: At the article were analyzed Byzantium sources of law made by non-clerical authority, which norms appeared in the Efremivska Kormcha. Also were researched volume and peculiarities of using this norms of law from Byzantium sources by editors of the indicated Kormcha list during its creation.
