Abstract: Natural legal school and historical legal school are considered to be antagonists as the former declares the universal nature of the legal phenomena for all the mankind and the latter stands for the idea of unique legal mechanisms of the small groups. However while analyzing the forming and development of the Romano-German legal family we can observe practical implementation of the concepts of both schools, which logically add each other and provide deeper perspective for analysis.
